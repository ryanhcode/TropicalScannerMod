# Tropical Scanner Official Mod
https://tropicalarmor.com/

Usage:
 - Download and use 1.8.9 forge
 - Put in mods folder
 - Log into hypixel and use `/tropical key <API_KEY>`(If you don't know how to get your API key, do `/api new` in hypixel)
 - Play normally and tropical will alert you of pieces for sale/pieces that the system hasn't seen before.
 - New pieces you find will be uploaded to the system after 10-15 minutes to give you a headstart.
 
 Download the JAR in the releases tab
